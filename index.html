Visualizing Representation on TikTok



You can select the data to be displayed here. Look below to find more information about each type of data.


First select the racial group you want to visualize.
Then for each characteristic of the spiral you can select a set of data.
There are five sets:

  • Top50_Comp - The representation of the racial group within the top 50 TikTokers as compared to that of the US population
  • Women_50 - the percentage of women included in the representaion of each group within the top 50
  • avg_Follower - the average follower count of the top TikTokers of the racial group
  • avg_comments - the average comment count of the top TikTokers
  • Comment_Rating - a positivity rating for the comments on the most recent videos of the top TikTokers of the group.
